02 · Access

Signing in as administrator

The admin account admin@toolvinementors.com is created and active on the platform. Vincent will share the Zoho mailbox credentials directly. Once you can read mail at that address, the platform sign-in is a single password reset.

  1. 1
    Open the Zoho mailbox

    The credentials will be sent to the founder's Gmail address. Sign in to Zoho Mail and you will be prompted to set your own password on first login.

    Open Zoho Mail
  2. 2
    Go to ToolVine sign-in

    Open the ToolVine sign-in page and choose "Forgot your password" below the form.

    Open ToolVine sign-in
  3. 3
    Enter admin@toolvinementors.com

    Submit the form. A password reset link is sent to that address, which lands in the Zoho inbox you just opened.

  4. 4
    Open the reset link, set your password

    Click the link in the Zoho email, choose a new password, sign in. You land on the admin dashboard with full access.

Why no password by email. Plaintext passwords sent over email are a soft security boundary. Sending you through the reset flow proves the platform's email pipeline works, gives you the credential you own from day one, and is the same path your members will use.
